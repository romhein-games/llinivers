Development Progress – Beta in Preparation

Development of the Owl Runner beta version is moving forward at full speed!

In recent weeks, Llinivers has focused on:
Improving the resource storage system, essential for enabling power-up purchases;
Adding new power-ups to enrich gameplay possibilities;
Polishing graphics to refine the game’s visual identity before the beta release.

The goal is clear: to deliver a solid, fun, and easy-to-pick-up beta, setting the stage for the upcoming final version.
