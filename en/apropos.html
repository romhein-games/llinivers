About Llinivers

Llinivers is, above all, a simple story: mine.

It all began alone, with a notebook full of ideas, a handful of raw dreams, and a deep desire to bring to life worlds that existed nowhere else. This unusual name is the one I chose to carry a vision: to create worlds where imagination can breathe, where you can lose yourself, and where you can find yourself again..

A micro-enterprise, a wide horizon

Today, Llinivers is a micro-enterprise — a small workshop where I craft, piece by piece, the interactive experiences that matter to me.

But behind its modest size lies a far greater ambition: to grow at the pace of its creations, to rise with every project, and to become, one day, a true studio. Not a soulless giant, but a creative home capable of giving life to games that are rich, deep, and daring.

Creating to Take You on a Journey

For now, I am developing my first mobile game: a playful, light experience designed to bring a first breath of life into the world of Llinivers.

This is only the beginning. A first flutter of wings. Because the vision is much bigger.

My goal is to design games that tell stories, that offer entire worlds, that leave a mark. More ambitious, more immersive games — experiences crafted for passionate players, whether they explore them on PC, console, or on more demanding platforms. Projects capable of competing with the most polished modern titles, without losing the artisanal soul that gave birth to the idea.

A Craft Approach, a Passion Undiminished

Alone at the helm, I move forward with patience, conviction, and curiosity. I code, I write, I design, I test, I erase, and I start over.

Each prototype, each line of code, each texture is my way of sculpting a world — a world that, I hope, will one day become yours.

This is Llinivers: a world in creation, an ambition on the rise.
